# SCN-017 — Before the docking hearing

**Story position:** Before the docking hearing  
**Date:** 14 October 2147

At 06:15, the evacuation was already underway and the lower corridors had emptied.

Mara sprinted across the north bridge, putting her full weight on her left ankle as the glass dust shifted beneath her boots. Ivo followed, one hand against the rail.

At the far door, Mara pulled the brass key from her pocket and turned it twice.

Captain Soren waited on the other side. Ivo gave him their old three-tap handshake.

“Still late,” Soren said.

“Still counting,” Ivo replied.

They entered the hearing chamber before the warning lamps changed.
