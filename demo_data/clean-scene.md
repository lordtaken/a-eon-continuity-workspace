# SCN-017B — Before the docking hearing

**Story position:** Before the docking hearing  
**Date:** 14 October 2147

At 06:45, the evacuation had begun and the lower corridors were clearing.

Mara moved along the south passage with her left ankle held off the floor, using Ivo’s shoulder only to keep balance. The north bridge remained behind a locked glass barrier.

They stopped outside the hearing chamber. The door required an internal release; the brass key was still sealed in Locker 12.

Captain Soren opened the door. Ivo studied him without recognition and offered his name.
